# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project is maintained for professional use in enterprise and air-gapped environments. All participants — contributors, issue reporters, and reviewers — are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive in all communications
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not individuals
- Accept that maintainer decisions are final on scope, direction, and acceptance
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks of any kind

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channel where project business is conducted.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **nimzshafie@gmail.com**. Reports are handled confidentially. The maintainer reserves the right to close issues or pull requests, remove comments, or ban participants who do not meet these standards without prior notice.

## No Entitlement

This is an open source project maintained by a single author. There is no obligation to respond to every issue, accept every contribution, or provide support on any particular timeline. Demanding responses or entitlement behavior will result in removal.
